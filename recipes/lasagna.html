Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is the world's best lasagna.The meal takes about 30 minutes to prepare and has a cook time of 2.5 hours at 375 degrees F.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions below
    1. Meat Sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat Sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then top the remaining Parmesan
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
